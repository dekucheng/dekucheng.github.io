Windmill Impact Simulation

This is the final project of ME314 (Machine Dynamics). The goal is to simulate the dynamic model of a system including one windmill and one block in Mathematica. The Lagrangian, Euler-Lagrangian and Hamiltonian equation are applied to derive the trajectory of the objects. To estimate if an impact happens, I set thirty-six impact conditions. When impact happens, the Impact-Update law is applied to update the velocity of each object and then use Lagrangian and EL-Lagrangian again to predict the trajectory of the system.
